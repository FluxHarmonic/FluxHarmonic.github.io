Flux Harmonic is a creative label for my projects, and a community for people who build their own tools, their own way, for their own use cases. It's for anyone with an idea they can't shake: the kind you have to make even when it seems foolish or infeasible.

My biggest challenge has always been having more ideas than I could ever build on my own. Now, with AI and my own ecosystem of tools, I've finally got a real shot at building what I always wanted to. Join me on this journey, and we'll figure out how to create authentically, even in the age of AI.
